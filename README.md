NTXautowerks: Rapid Rebrand & SEO Recovery
Project Overview
This project involved a full visual rebrand and a strategic SEO recovery for NTXautowerks, a client who experienced a significant loss in Google ranking after a relocation and rebranding effort. The solution leveraged an AI-driven workflow to rebuild their digital presence and achieve a #1 organic Google ranking with zero ad spend.
The Challenge
After 15 years as a client, NTXautowerks rebranded and relocated, which unfortunately led to the loss of their first-page Google ranking and diminished online visibility.
The Hybrid Solution
I executed a comprehensive visual rebrand aligned with their new identity and implemented an updated tech stack for their website. The core of the solution was a custom AI-driven workflow that rapidly rebuilt the site and significantly enhanced its SEO.
Strategic Result
The AI-powered approach enabled the site to regain and surpass its previous search performance, achieving a #1 organic Google ranking within a few days, demonstrating exceptional efficiency and impact without any advertising expenditure.
AI & Technical Transparency
Workflow: Automated Pipeline (Multi-Model CLI Automation)
Tools: React, Next.js, Tailwind CSS, Firebase Deployment (Resulting in a 3.13MB deployment)

SEO & Digital Marketing: Achieving top organic search rankings and maximizing online visibility.
